Betterworldbooks

Better World Books is an American online bookseller of used and new books, founded in 2002 by students of the University of Notre Dame, Indiana.Better World Books' used book inventory comes primarily from regular book drives at over 1,800 colleges and universities and donations from over 3,000 library systems, in addition to donation boxes found on corners and on college campuses.The company has distribution warehouses in Mishawaka, Indiana; Reno, Nevada; and Dunfermline, Scotland.
